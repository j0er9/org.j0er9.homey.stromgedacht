Monitor the power grid status for Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany with real-time data and forecasts. Automate your devices based on grid load (Super Green, Green, Yellow, Red) to reduce energy consumption during peak times.

Example: Charge your electric car when the grid status is Super Green, or pause charging when it turns Red.

This is an independent third-party app and has no affiliation with the official Stromgedacht service, TransnetBW GmbH, or Stromnetz Berlin GmbH.
